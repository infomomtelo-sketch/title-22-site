What the walkthrough covers

01

Morning briefing

Log in and see what needs attention today — flagged in plain language before your first cup of coffee.

02

Administering and documenting a dose

A caregiver opens their shift, sees the resident's scheduled medications, and documents administration in seconds — no paper MAR, no end-of-shift scramble.

03

Handling a PRN dose

Record the reason, the dose given, and a same-shift follow-up note — the three things an analyst looks for every time.

04

Administrator view across facilities

Switch between licensed facilities, review caregiver activity, and spot documentation gaps before they become citations.

A hard line we don't cross: nowhere in this walkthrough does the AI suggest, adjust, or comment on a dosage. It flags missing documentation — the clinical call always stays with the physician and pharmacist.
